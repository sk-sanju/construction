Our Timeline

How We Work

We follow a structured phase-by-phase execution model to maintain transparency and standard construction schedules.

1

Consultation

Initial meeting, site review, budget determination, client needs assessment, and architectural feasibility check.

2

Planning

Structural calculations, legal construction permissions layout, estimator cost breakdown, material selections schedule.

3

Design

Stunning 3D elevations, exterior/interior rendering walk-throughs, Vasthu correction, floor plans validation.

4

Construction

Ground breaking, structural frame foundation, RCC columns casting, brick masonry layout, wiring and plumbing installation.

5

Quality Inspection

Rigorous materials strength validation, finishing verification, load assessment, waterproofing review, final paint coating.

6

Project Delivery

Handing over the keys, clean up and finishing touchups, customer induction to structural layout elements.
